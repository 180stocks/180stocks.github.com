180 Stocks : Day 33 (Jubilant Life Sciences)

Mon 03 February 2014

I was going through this report on the pharmaceutical sector in India. One of the names that came up in the report was of Jubilant Life Sciences.

Recently, there have been a few USFDA concerns about the company, along with forex losses, impaiment of goodwill and many other negatives have contributed a loss of Rs. Crores during the last couple of years. Consequently the market capitalization of the company too has fallen from Rs. Crores to Rs. 2,145 Crores.

This attracted me to answer the question:

Is the company "long established, well financed, important in its industry and presumably destined to stay in business and make profits indefinitely in the future, but that have no speculative or growth appeal?"

Jubilant Life Sciences serves its customers globally with sales in more than 99 countries and ground presence in India, North America, Europe and China. We are well recognised as a ‘Partner of Choice’ by leading life sciences companies worldwide. Jubilant has relationship with 19 of Top 20 pharmaceutical companies and 6 out of the top 10 agrochemical companies across the globe. 1

Has a network of 7 world class manufacturing facilities in India and 3 in North America and a team of over 6300 people across the globe with ~ 1400 in North America and ~1000 in R&D. 1

High debt: Proposed IPO in Singapore Gross block net of goodwill: 3800 cr

"Too good to be true, it often is!"
